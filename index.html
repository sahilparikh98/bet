Welcome to Bet!

Bet is a social betting app that allows you to create and send bets to your friends over anything! Whether it's a football game, how a TV show finale is going to end, or who gets a higher GPA at the end of the semester, make it fun and bet on it!

Intelligent Request System

Bet! uses an extensive request system encourages friendly user interactions. Everything requires both users to approve, so you can assure that you are completely in control of your bets.

Facebook Integration

Bet your Facebook friends by signing in with Facebook!
